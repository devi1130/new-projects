DEGA RESTAURANT

A restaurant is a place where people go to enjoy food. It serves many types of tasty dishes and drinks. People visit restaurants with their family and friends. The staff in the restaurant are friendly and help customers. A good restaurant is clean, comfortable, and makes people happy.

Chocolate creamy cake is very soft and delicious. It is made with rich chocolate and smooth cream. Many people love this cake for celebrations and special days.
Cookies are crunchy and tasty snacks. They are baked with flour, sugar, and butter. People enjoy cookies with tea or milk.
Ice cream is a cold and sweet dessert. It comes in many flavors like chocolate and vanilla. Everyone loves ice cream on hot days.
Homemade chocolate is sweet and delicious. It is prepared with love at home. It is healthier and fresher than store chocolate.
Fresh juice is healthy and refreshing. It is made from natural fruits. It gives energy and keeps the body cool.
Puffs are crispy snacks filled with vegetables or meat. They are hot and tasty. Many people enjoy puffs in the evening.
Pani puri is a very popular street food. It is crispy and spicy. Many people love eating it.Roasted corn is a healthy street food. It is spicy and tasty. It is popular in the evening.
Faluda is a sweet and refreshing dessert drink. It is made with milk, rose syrup, vermicelli, and ice cream. Many people enjoy faluda during hot weather.
